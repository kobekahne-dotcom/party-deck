How to play Mirror

Golden rule: you drew it, you do it. Chicken out = you drink.

Move cards (dances, scramble, wave): on 3·2·1 — mirror! everyone does it at once. Fumble, hesitate, or do it wrong → drink.

"Who drinks" cards (tallest, wearing black): the card says who — that person drinks.

Task cards (read your notification, do an impression): the person who drew it does it — or drinks instead.

A COMBO card means do the last cards in order first, then the new one — a memory chain that gets harder the longer you play.

How to play Call It

Deal a card — it's a task for one person (drawer picks, or go around).

Before they try, on 3·2·1 — call it! everyone else throws a thumb up (they'll make it) or thumb down (they'll miss).

They make it → everyone who called down drinks. They miss → the attempter drinks and everyone who called up drinks.

Test it at a real party. Watch one thing: does anyone ask to play again, or teach it to someone who wasn't playing. That's the whole signal.
